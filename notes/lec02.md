# Linear Algebra

## Cross Product

$\vec{a}\times\vec{b}= A^*\vec{b}$

$A^*=\begin{pmatrix}
    0 & -z_a & y_a \\
    z_a & 0 & -x_a \\
    -y_a & x_a & 0 \\
\end{pmatrix}$

## Orthonormal Coordinate Frames

$\vec{p}=(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{u})\vec{u}+(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{v})\vec{v}+(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{w})\vec{w}$
